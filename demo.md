## The Assignment Prompt

> Take one of the primary sources for this week — King, Eccles, Davis, or Carter — and use
> it to explain *why* the Vietnam War proved so corrosive to the New Deal Order. Answer in no
> more than two double-spaced pages. Your answer should include well-chosen quotes or examples
> from both your primary source and the Gerstle reading to substantiate your central argument.
